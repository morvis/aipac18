Airport Ground Transportation:
There are multiple transportation options for getting to and from all three of the Washington, D.C., metro area’s major airports. SuperShuttle offers service to all three airports and operates 24 hours a day. Contact 1-800-BLUEVAN (1-800-258-3826) or www.supershuttle.com for reservations.

If you are arriving at Reagan Washington National Airport, you can reach the Washington Convention Center using D.C. Metro’s Yellow line.

RMA Worldwide Chauffeured Transportation:
RMA is a premier transportation company that is available in more than 600 cities across the country. AIPAC has arranged a preferred rate for chauffeured sedans and SUVs to and from local D.C. airports. You may contact RMA at (800) 878-7743 or email reservations@rmalimo.com to set up your travel plans. RMA is open 24/7. When making your reservations, please use the code “Policy Conference 2018” to ensure that you receive the preferred rate.

Additional Transportation:
Washington, D.C., is one of the nation’s most accessible cities. The Washington Convention Center is conveniently located near several parking facilities. The Washington Convention Center is accessible using the D.C. Metro Yellow and Green lines. Taxi and Uber service are available throughout the metropolitan area.
