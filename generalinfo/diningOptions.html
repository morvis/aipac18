Dining Options

All food served during the conference, with the exception of certain retail outlets in the Marriott Marquis, is kosher and supervised by the Capitol-K Rabbinical Council of Greater Washington. Your personalized schedule will show if any of your scheduled meetings and programs include meals.

Washington Convention Center:
A wide range of food and beverages will be available for purchase using a credit card, grab-and-go meal or a meal voucher. Vouchers for breakfast and lunch may be purchased at the Conference Concierge in the AIPAC Village, which will accept cash and credit cards.

Check back closer to the conference for details on food and beverage options in the AIPAC Village, Uptown Food District, and AIPAC Coffee House.

Retail Food and Beverage Menus and Hours: The Washington Convention Center
Details about menus will be available soon.

The Marriott Marquis:
The Minyan Lounge (open to Minyan members only) will serve kosher food from Saturday at 3:00 pm until Tuesday at 10:00 am. The Anthem restaurant and M Club Lounge will serve kosher food starting Sunday at 6:00 am. Room service will have kosher and non-kosher options available. High Velocity Sports Bar, the Lobby Bar, and Starbucks are not kosher.
